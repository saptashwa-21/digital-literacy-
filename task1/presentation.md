## Block 1 – What is Digital Literacy?

Ability to find, evaluate & use digital information
Knowing which tools to use for which task
Being responsible online

## Block 2 – Safe Internet Practices

Use strong, unique passwords
Don't click unknown links
Enable two-factor authentication
Never share OTP or UPI PIN

## Block 3 – Professional Online Presence

Keep social media clean & professional
Set up LinkedIn with your education details
Use your college email for sign-ups
Think before you post — the internet never forgets
